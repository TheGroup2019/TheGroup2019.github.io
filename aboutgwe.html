Girls Who Experienced

Girls Who Experienced. As a final project for the Girls Who Code, 7-week summer Immersion program, we decided to make Girls Who Experienced. It is a website in where we tell our experiences and stories to future girls and coders who would participate in the program. As we were thinking about our final projects, we realized that it would have been beneficial to us if someone would have told us what to expect. To make your lives easier, we decided to create a website!

Girls Who Code is a nonprofit organization which aims to support and increase the number of women in computer science. They have a 7-week Summer Immersion Program, a 2-week specialized Campus Program, after school Clubs, and a 13-book New York Times best-selling series. The goal is to inspire, educate, and equip young women with the computing skills to pursue 21st century opportunities.
