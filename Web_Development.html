Build a Web Page


image source=https://www.websitebuilderexpert.com/building-websites/

As you can tell by the title, you're going to build a website. It's quite easy and it involves a lot of writing. You begin by designing your page on paper. Come up with an idea for it. If you don't have any, here are some. A page of your favorite food, tv show, and game. Anything goes for a website! once you finish, you now go on Atom and type out your idea. Once you finished and saved your file, click on it, on the files application, and feast your eyes on your first website! Then create a css file to make your page better. This is my favorite project of all time, because to me, it was amazing building a website.

Web Dev for the day


image source=https://www.quora.com/What-is-the-difference-between-web-development-web-design

For this project, you and a partner are developing websites for each other. After exchanging ideas, you begin designing one another's websites. I had so much with this project, because the website my partner wanted was really cool and funny. You don't need a professional or a perfect website for your partner. All you need is a working one that she asked for. Remember to check on each other's websites. As the client, be sure to check that your developer is doing their best for yor website. As the developer, be sure you're putting your best efforts on her website as well. You both need a good working website.
