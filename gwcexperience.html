Here are our experiences and feedbacks from the Girls Who Code program.

Experience:

In the girls who code program we went on a total of three field trips, three mentor panels and 5+ guest speakers. For the field trips we went to a Royal Caribbean Cruise, Venture City, and the University of Miami. For the field trips we got to go on a actual cruise and ate lunch on the cruise. It was cool, except for the part that they didn't let us go into the pool. The other field trip we went to an office, but it was a really cool office. It had unicorns, iguanas, skateboards and two dogs. Other than all that fun stuff they showed us how to come up with an effective way to brainstorm for ideas. The last field trip we went to the university of Miami. It was cool, they gave us a tour of the university and we also got to visit the 3D printing lab and we got to play with Magic Leap.

The mentor panel consisted of women, although most of them didn't hold any tech experience. However they provided usefull advice that will stick with you for the rest of your life. Also you learn new terms like bio breaks. The guest speakers, unlike the mentors, most of the speakekr held tech positions. The were nice some of them gave us stickers or water bottles. The guest speaker that stood most was Chirstina Kelly.

Overall this program was really fun and it will be an experience that will stick with us for the rest of our lives. The topics we learned and the advice we receive will help us in the future as we pursue our dreams.
